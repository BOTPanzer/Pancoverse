GTX Usb Carrying apps and games in a USB isn't a bad idea, but the memory's speed can cause problems. To fix this I had the idea of adding a cooler, so i started designing case which ended up having the shape of an Nvidia GTX graphics card.

After adding the holes for the ventilation, I printed the case and placed a small fan that gets powered from a little connector on the side. Finally, I put a thermal pad and an aluminium plate, which was later painted, to dissipate heat from the memory. 3D Modeling 路 3D Printing 路 Electronics 路 DIY
Discord Bots After checking out the cool things you can do with discord bots I got the idea of creating my own.

At first I created a simple bot called Syrup and, after becoming better at making them, I created a more advanced version called Coscu. This new version could jump into a call with people and play sounds so I used Adobe Audition to edit them and make them sound better.
